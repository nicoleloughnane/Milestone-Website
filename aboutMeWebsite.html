My Hobbies

Hobbies Year(s) Description
Art 2003-present

I've had an interest in art ever since I was 2 years old. I started with coloring existing art and then made pieces of my own with crayons and watercolors. I took a few art classes in school. Now it is something I do in my free time. I make holiday and birthday cards for my friends and family.

Guitar 2014-present

I learned acoustic guitar when I was in junior high school. I like to play country, rock, and pop songs. Even though I have been on and off with the hobby, I was able to learn enough to start a YouTube channel of some songs I learned, which helps track my progress. This past summer I even got an electric guitar. You can find my videos on my things to share page.

Golf 2015-present

Starting my freshman year of high school, I was on the girls golf team. I played until my junior year and went to competitions with other schools. In my free time I like to go practice and teach my friends how to play.

Computer Programming 2017-present

My junior year of high school is when I started taking an interest in computer science. I took some AP classes that year and in senior year which is when I discovered what I wanted to do. I majored in computer science at my community college but transferred to software development (formerly computer programming) at GCU! I have had most experience in Java and C# but am quickly building up knowledge in other areas.
